Responder
If a client/target cannot resolve a name via DNS it will fall back to name resolution via LLMNR (introduced in Windows Vista) and NBT-NS.
Responder is a tool we can utilize for exploiting the LLMNR and NBT-NS weakness for capturing NTLMv1/v2 hashes and relaying them for authentication to other systems.
Responder works by listening for LLMNR or NBT-NS broadcast messages, and spoofing responses to targeted hosts, resulting in intercepting hashes and act as a Man in the Middle.
