Legal Paper Work




Penetration testers need to know the legal context and requirements around their work in addition to the technical and process portions of a penetration test.
Contracts, statementsof work, NDAs, and the laws and legal requirements each state, country, or local jurisdiction enforces are all important to know and understand before starting a penetration test
