5. Contractual Agreements


This is the part of legal paperwork


Statement of Work (SOW)


Before you offer Penetration services, you may need to write a Statement of Work (SOW) that outlines the work you are going to perform
To writing a Statement of Work (SOW) check the Project Scope that you agreed with the Client

Master Services Agreement (MSA)


Drawn up by Lawyer. A good example of a complete MSA from Rapid7: https://www.rapid7.com/legal/msa/
• It is absolutely vital that you get a Permission to test (called also Permission Memo or "Get Out of Jail Free Card" aka GOOJFC ) from a leader of the target organization giving us the permission to test its environment.
As a starting point we can use this sample memo: http://www.counterhack.net/permission_memo.html
*Permission Memo and Rules of Engagement are two different things
• We have also include a limitations of liability agreement that caps the liability for any damage associated with the penetration test at the price of the project itself.
Thus if the penetration test cost 50000$ the limitation of the liability agreement caps the liability at that value.
In addition we can get a professional insurance(sometime required by the clients) that covers errors and omission from the penetration testing team. Common insurance levels range between 1$-5$ million

Follow the Law


• When performing testing a country where we have not tested before, we have to consult our lawyer.
• The tester has to follow not only the laws where he or she is located but also the laws in the country where the target machines are located
◇ Sometime also the laws of the states(such USA) that the the packets of our traffic traverse
• The text of the cybercrime laws of more than 100 countries is been gathered here: https://www.unodc.org/cld/v3/cybrepo/legdb/


Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA)


Because during the pentesting work we can came across confidential data, company usually ask to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDA). With that company ensures that we will not divulge any information.
