Response Strategies
One thing to avoid Is having an incident happen and finding a debate about what to do:
◇ Maintain secrecy OR notify law enforcement
▪ Most organizations maintain secrecy until they must notify law enforcement (That's not always the best policy, though)
◇ Contain the incident and clean up OR watch the attackers and try to gather more evidence (see https://www.honeynet.org/)
▪ Most organizations have a default pre-authorization to contain, but may handle it differently depending on the particulars of the case
The time to make these decisions is before the incident, keeping senior
management and the legal staff apprised.
