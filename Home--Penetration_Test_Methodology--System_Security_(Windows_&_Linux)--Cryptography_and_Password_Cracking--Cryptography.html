Cryptography



Cryptography (or cryptology) is the art of communicating secretly
encryption is the process of transforming a plaintext message into a ciphertext.
ciphertext is not understandable unless we are in possession of the decryption key.
decryption is the process of retrieving the original plaintext message from a ciphertext by using the decryption key

Cryptography is based on four fundamental pillars:


• Authentication: claims made by or about the subject are true
• Confidentiality: information is accessible only to those authorized to have access
• Integrity: message has not been manipulated/altered in the transfer
• Non-repudiation: ensuring that a party in a dispute cannot repudiate, or refute the validity of a statement
