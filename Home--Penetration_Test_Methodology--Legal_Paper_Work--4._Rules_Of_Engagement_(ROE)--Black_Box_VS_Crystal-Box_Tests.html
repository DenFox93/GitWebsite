Black Box VS Crystal-Box Tests


Why do a Black Box Testing
◇ More like the real-world attackers? This is not necessarily true. A real-world attacker might have a picture of your network architecture,stolen for example from an employee.
◇ I a network diagram have significant limitations or errors, it may bias penetration testers that can miss details and sometimes overlooking hosts or whole networks

Why do a Crystal Box Testing(usually recomendeed than Black Box Testing)
◇ more cost effective because the attackers can quickly perform their reconnaissance and scanning
◇ have a network diagram lets the penetration testing team analyze a worst-case scenario, where the attacker have it
◇ lowered chance of an error that can cause damage to an out-of-scope system

Hybrid Testing(best)
The testing team starts out with a Black Box Testing with nothing more than a domain name and performs detailed recon and scanning.
Then after a week or so, we can start a Crystal Box Testing and the target organization provides a detailed diagram of the network for further testing and analysis.
CONS:
◇ Hybrid tests usually take a little longer and therefore cost more
