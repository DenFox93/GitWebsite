Team Organization


Define incident handling team organization:
• Technically oriented "action" team members go to the site of the incident, collect data, evaluate the situation, and make decisions (or make recommendations, depending on their site's philosophy).
• communications work needs to be done through coordination with other groups, and there are times when the decisions that need to be made must involve upper management.

Establish a response time baseline:
• Response time may vary (e. g. 15 to 90 minutes) depending on sensitivity
• Have a skilled person respond within N minutes at all major facilities

Emergency Communication Plan
Develop an Emergency Communications Plan:
• Create a call list and establish methods of informing people quickly
• A shared voice mailbox fol the core team allows each member access and is strongly recommended
• you should print a credit card sized list of incident response team contact informatlon. Include each team member's name, role, phone number, fax number, and PGP key fingerprint (if you are using PGP). Pass out this credit card-sized list to each member of the team.
