Incident Handling


Incident handling is the action or plan for dealing with
• intrusions
• cyber theft
• denial-of-servicer

The best way to act on an incident and minimize your chance of a mistake is by having proper procedures in place.
Well documented procedures ensure that you know what to do when an incident occurs and minimize the chances that you will forget something

Incident Handling Process


In incident handling there are 6 steps:
1. Preparation
2. Identification
3. Containment
4. Eradication
5. Recovery
6. Lessons Learned

Anyway the steady state (day-to-day practices) of most incident handlers are the first two steps:
◇ Preparation
◇ Identification
After we identify an incident (that is, events that indicate harm or the attempt to do harm), we move into Containment.
