DNP3 (DNP 3.0)


DNP3 was primarily designed for electrical industry
• Supported functions include:
◇ Send request
◇ Accept response
◇ Confirmation, timeouts, error recovery.
• SCADA/EMS applications
◇ RTU to IED communications
◇ Master to remote communications.
• Work most of the time on Port 20000 (standard but it can be changed)
• Also available DNP over UDP 
• DNP3 Secure Authentication
