Take Notes


Not take notes is one of the most common, least excusable errors incident handlers make.

How long should you keep your records? In general, keep them as long as possible

Take excellent notes in a bound notebook with numbered pages. The joumalist's standard questions of who what where when and why are a bare minimum.
It is important to record all of your actions including the questions you asked the answers you received the commands you typed the systems you downed...
