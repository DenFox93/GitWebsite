Executive Summary



In the Executive Summary we have to describe to managers of the client company what the results of the report mean, and more importantly, recommendations for the actions they need to take based on its results.
Effectively communicating the results to management is critical because they will be the managers to take decisions.

• Although it appears at the start of the report, is recommend to write it for last, after that we have completed the rest of the report
• It should be quite short, not more than one page.
• It should not be highly technical because can be read by managers that could not be very technical
• It should offer conclusions(should not go over every finding) and advice for someone who doesn't necessarily read the rest of the document

Points that should be included


• It should start with a brief description of the project:
◇ describe the project's date and goals
◇ who participated in the project
◇ provide a brief overview of what is been done.
• Overall risk posture of the target environment
◇ major problems discovered
◇ Environment is worse or better than expected by testers and/or Client
• List of the biggest three to six findings
◇ explain the business impact in terms of the risk for these findings
◇ Possible changes at management level:
▪ organizational structure
▪ altering policies and procedures
▪ adding personnel
▪ deploy a new technology
▪ ..
